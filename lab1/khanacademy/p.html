Pancakes

  • Time: 15 minutes
  • Serves: 2
Ingredients Quantity
Milk cup
Sugar 2 small spoon
Egg 3
Flour cup

Step 1: Mix flour, sugar, and baking powder in a bowl.

Step 2: Add egg and milk, then stir until smooth.

Step 3: Pour batter into a hot pan and cook both sides.

Salad

  • Time: 20 minutes
  • Serves: 3
Ingredients Quantity
Cooked chicken 2 cups
Lettuce 1 head
Tomatoes 2

Steps:

  1. Chop chicken and vegetables.
  2. Mix everything in a bowl.
  3. Add olive oil and salt, then toss.

Chocolate Chip Cookies

  • Time: 25 minutes
  • Serves: 4
Ingredients Quantity
Flour 2 cups
Butter 1 cup
Sugar 1 cup
Eggs 2
Chocolate chips 1 cup

Steps:

  1. Mix butter and sugar.
  2. Add eggs, then flour.
  3. Fold in chocolate chips.
  4. Bake at 180°C for 12–15 minutes.
